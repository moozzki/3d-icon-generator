# Refund Policy - Audora

At Audora, we strive to provide the best AI-powered 3D icon generation experience. Because our system incurs immediate costs from our AI providers (Fal.ai) and infrastructure when generating high-quality assets, we have a clear policy regarding refunds.

## 1. Credit Purchases
**All credit purchases are final.** Once you have purchased a credit package (Starter, Creator, or Studio) and the credits have been added to your account, we are unable to offer a refund. 

By purchasing credits, you acknowledge that:
- Credits are digital goods that are consumed upon use.
- Credits have no cash value and cannot be exchanged back for currency once delivered.
- You have had the opportunity to test the service using the initial **2 Free Credits** provided upon sign-up.

## 2. Technical Failures & Credit Returns
We distinguish between a **Cash Refund** (returning money to your original payment method) and a **Credit Return** (returning credits to your Audora balance).

- **Automated Credit Returns:** If a generation job fails due to a system error or is blocked by content safety filters, the credits used for that specific task will be automatically returned to your account balance.
- **Failed Generations:** In the rare event that a generation completes but the result is corrupted or unusable due to a technical glitch, please contact support for a manual credit adjustment.

## 3. Payment Issues
Refunds to the original payment method may only be considered in the following cases:
- **Duplicate Transactions:** If you were charged multiple times for a single order due to a technical error in the payment gateway (**Pakasir**).
- **Non-Delivery:** If your payment was successful but credits were not added to your account, and we are unable to resolve the delivery issue manually.

## 4. Contact Us
If you encounter any issues with your purchase or believe you are eligible for a refund under the specific exceptions listed above, please contact our support team.

- **Email:** support@useaudora.com
- **Details Needed:** Transaction ID, account email, and a description of the issue.
- **Support Hours:** 8 AM - 4 PM WIB
